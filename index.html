Assam is the main and oldest state in the northern region of india and serves as the gateway to rest of the seven sisters. A large number of tourist visit every year to assam.The state is mainly famous for its natural beauty along with the greenary of its tea gardens.For the purpose of tourism their are wildlife reserves like kaziranga national park,Manas national park, pobitara wildlife sanctuary,Namery national park,Dibru saikhowa national park etc.other than that assam is also the home to the great indian one horned Rhino,Muga silk and world's largest river island Majuli.Their are several tourist places with historical and cultural importance such as Sivasagar,Tezpur,Guwahati,Majuli,Goalpara etc.In my project i would like to show the historical significance of Sivasagar.
